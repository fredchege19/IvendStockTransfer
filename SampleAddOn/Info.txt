This sample add-on shows how to handle button events on Management Console screen. e.g. Before/After Ok/Cancel events

* For Ok button, Before and after events are exposed as OnBeforeSave and OnAfterSave.

* For Cancel button, Before and after events are exposed as OnBeforeCancel and OnAfterCancel.

e.g. For Customer AddEdit View screen, Before and After events of Ok and Cancel button is demonstrated in Customer.cs
